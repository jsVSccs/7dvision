Face Detection and Face Tracking

Through face detection, face alignment, face recognition process, viface can easily extract the facial feature points from the image / video / camera, and perform the photo-to-avatar, face reconstruction and accurate face tracking.

Rapid 3D Face Rescontruction

viface utilizes the face tracking, blendshape / Morphing process to transform smoothly from one human face to another 3D human face.

Realtime animation/streaming

Applying blendshape technology on the 3D avatar model with the generated facial expression weights from viface, viface supports the real time streaming and import / export of the face animation data to Maya / Unity / Unreal.
