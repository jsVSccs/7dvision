vipano

Realtime video stitching software, vipano supports SDI/HDMI/USB as video inputs, and SDI/RTMP/HDD recording as video output. Also, vipano supports different types of camera rigs, from two lens to 12 lens camera matrix. As the stitching calibration methods, we supports third party tool, PTGUI, and our proprietary chess board calibration method. Vipano not only supports monoscopic 360 video, but also supports stereoscopic 360 and stereoscopic 180 video format.
